Stardate Converter

Misc. notes

  • Uses the TNG-era(-onward, incl. DISs3 etc.) stardate system, where 2323 = stardate 0.
    • TOS-era stardates are just sorta random.
    • For some reason, Lower Decks uses a variant on this. I haven't found what the formula is, but it's just a difference of a few months.
  • The stardates in the "LAST REVISION" sections in the top left use 2000 as the base instead of 2323.
  • JavaScript has a maximum date of Sep 13, 275760 and cannot represent dates after that.
  • The "Stardate → Earth" outputs in the format yyyy-mm-dd hh:mm:ss.
  • You can append ?s= followed by a stardate to the URL to automatically convert that stardate.
  • The input boxes pre-fill with the current UTC time.
    • This makes sense for the Federation because
      • it's already standardized
      • it is called Universal Coordinated Time (though the initialism is broken because French)
    • Here's a converter. (Don't know what your UTC offset is? Search your location on time.is and scroll down a bit.)
