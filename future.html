The future of Star Trek is uncertain.

The writers' and actors' guilds are both on an extended strike currently, so any new Star Trek things that haven't already wrapped production are essentially off the table until the strikes end. And Paramount+ is shelving some shows (in general) for tax write-offs. So our best guess for the future is this:
