VOY novels • Full Circle

In which Chakotay reminisces over the past two and a half years, and Voyager is sent back into the Delta Quadrant with a fleet.

Plot summary

June 2380. Chakotay is about to meet with Janeway in-person, something they'd scheduled a year prior. But instead, Mark (to whom she was engaged before Voyager was lost in the DQ) shows up. He says Janeway isn't coming, because she is dead.

September 2378. B'Elanna Torres has spent the past eight months on Boreth trying to determine the fate of her daughter Miral, who some believe is the quvamaq, fated to save the Empire and restore the Klingon gods – which B'Elanna finds ridiculous. She calls Tom Paris, mentioning that Miral is "trying something new tonight" (sleeping); he responds by saying his mom had invited them both to a two-month-late celebration of his promotion to first officer.

After Tom hangs up, he realizes his dad had been listening in. Owen worries about him missing Miral's growing up. Tom then remembers some Captain Eden had wanted to see him.
